Gopal Kris Santhana

Founder, Managing Partner

Gopal "Kris" Santhana is the founder and managing partner of a high growth company providing digital experiences and technology services. As a trusted technology advisor, he guides and advises CTOs on their digital strategy. He has previously held technology leadership roles at Fortune 100 companies including Amazon, Discover Financial Services, Walgreens and HSBC. Prior to that he was providing technology consultancy for major brands lncluding Allstate, KPMG and JPMorgan Chase. Gopal "Kris" Santhana is an alumnus of Oklahoma State University and has a Bachelor of Engineering from Guindy Engineering College, Anna University.
